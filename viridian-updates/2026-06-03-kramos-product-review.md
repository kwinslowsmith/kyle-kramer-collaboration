## The Meta Pattern

Kyle's building a **relational operating system** — not just task management, but:

1. **People as the center** (not projects)
2. **Multi-domain coordination** (improv, K-12, personal)
3. **Observation as data** (what makes people effective, where they're growing)
4. **Timeline visibility** (when things happen, how long they take)
5. **Community/team structure** (how people relate, which teams matter)

This feels like the **digital backbone for orchestration at scale** — exactly what both Viridian and KRAMOS need. A place to see who, what, when, and why.
